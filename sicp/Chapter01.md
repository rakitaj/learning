## 1.2.4 Exponentiation
Computing an exponent by successive squaring vs the typical recursive process is much quicker.

Standard
b^8 = b * b * b * b * b * b * b * b

Successive squaring
b^8 = b^4 * b^4
b^4 = b^2 * b^2
b^2 = b * b
